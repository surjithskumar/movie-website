About the Film

MAHARAJA

Maharaja is a quiet man, working as a barber in Ramki Saloon and living in Pallikaranai, Chennai. One day, a truck slams into his house, leading to his wife's death. His daughter, Jothi, survives when a dustbin accidentally falls and covers her. Maharaja and Jothi name the dustbin "Lakshmi" out of affection. Jothi later travels out of the city for a sports camp in Coimbatore with her sports teacher. Meanwhile, Maharaja visits the local police station to report a robbery, citing that Lakshmi has been robbed, much to the amusement of the cops. He recounts the incident: an armed gang raided his house, attacked him, and took Lakshmi.
